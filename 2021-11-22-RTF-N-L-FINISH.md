### What is a meme?

- Imitable
- Transformable
- Participatory
- Polysemic

- Demotivational posters
- Single-serving sites

### Functions

### What is a gif?
- Short, looping moving image
- Origins in 19th century devices
- Easily searchable
- Embody emotions

- Signifier
